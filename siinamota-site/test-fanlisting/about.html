who is siinamota?

siinamota (椎名もた) - real name: mizoguchi ryo - was born on march 09, 1995 and he is a japanese artists! he mostly composed and produced vocaloid songs mainly featuring hatsune miku throughout his career. he also used other vocaloids such as kagamine rin, gumi, and megurine luka. his discography is full of energetic j-pop and j-rock songs that are heavily influenced by electronic genres.

writing and composing music since he was only 14 years old, he managed to release his first album in 2010 under the label karent named sepia record. as his music grew attention so did his career. he got signed under the label ginga as a composer and producer while also helping him release the rest of his discography.

while he was mostly known for his music, he also liked drawing and would feature his art on album covers and booklets. he would also share his doodles on social media. he has also collabed with fashion lines for album releases such as alterour setsuna pop where he collabed with earth music ＆ecology japan label which included clothes and accesories designed by him and one of his main album artists: meisa

ryo passed away at age 20, on july 23, 2015. the cause of his death is undisclosed though it is heavily believed to be because of suicide due to nature of his postings that same day.

some other names siinamota has gone by thoughout his music career has been: fulkawa noro, powapowa-P, and highcolorz_sun
